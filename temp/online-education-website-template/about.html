Dream Path

DreamPath Foundation is a non-profit organisation operating from Bengaluru. Our work is centred around career awareness and guidance. OUR VISION

Our vision is a society where all youth are sufficiently enabled to seek an occupation or vocation of their choice, ability, aptitude and means, thereby participating actively in its development and progress as a whole. Towards this end, it is our effort to

  • Create an informed, collaborative ecosystem of students, parents, teachers and institutions that positively affects career decisions of youth
  • Seek and collaborate with partners and / or policy makers who can help further our mission
  • Reach youth all over the country through workshops, programs, publications, expert assistance and technology
We have a multi-pronged approach to achieve these goals:

Our various activities and services are geared to meet the following objectives:

  • Facilitate self-assessment for students, in order to accurately identify interests and aptitude
  • Increase awareness of possible professions and corresponding education paths
  • Promote communication between guardian and child
  • Enable and encourage informed rather than ad hoc decision making
  • Provide a realistic view of careers and corresponding work-life
  • Enable interaction with professionals and experts in various fields
  • Inculcate appreciation of every profession and job and instill an unbiased view
